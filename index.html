NFL Draft – Round 1 Predictions

Test your Mock Draft skills against experts and your friends. Submit your predictions for all 32 picks in Round 1 of the NFL Draft.

Rules & Scoring

  • 3 points for an exact match (correct player at the correct pick)
  • 1 point if the player is drafted anywhere in Round 1
  • 0 points if the player is not drafted in Round 1

Predictions are locked before the draft begins. Scores and rankings will update live during the draft.
